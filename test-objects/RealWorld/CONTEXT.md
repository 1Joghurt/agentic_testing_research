The application to be tested is the online blog “Conduit”. It is a social publishing platform for people who want to read articles, write their own articles, or interact with other authors. The core idea is similar to a simple blogging platform: content is publicly readable, while publishing and social features require a user account.

Articles consist of a title, a short description, the main content, and optional tags. Readers can discover posts generally, by topic, or based on authors they follow. Free full-text search for articles is not provided.

Registered users can maintain a profile with a username, image, and biography, and they can change their login credentials. They can publish their own articles and subsequently edit or delete them. Articles written by others can be favorited and commented on. Favorited posts are associated with the user's own profile and can be found there again.

Users can also follow other authors, which causes those author's publications to be included in a personalized content selection. Favorites and following relationships can be removed at any time. Users can delete their own comments, but they cannot edit them.

The platform does not support private messages or collaborative editing of articles. Visitors without an account cannot publish, comment, favorite articles, or follow other users. In addition, users may only modify or remove their own articles and comments.
